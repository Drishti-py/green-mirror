# 🌍 GreenMirror

### *Your world reflects your footprint.*

GreenMirror is an AI-powered sustainability platform that transforms carbon footprint tracking into an immersive, living digital experience.

Instead of showing users only numbers and charts, GreenMirror visualizes environmental impact through a dynamic ecosystem that evolves based on real-world consumption patterns.

As emissions increase, the ecosystem becomes polluted with smog, dark clouds, and declining biodiversity.

As sustainable actions are taken, forests flourish, rivers recover, wildlife returns, and the environment becomes vibrant again.

GreenMirror helps users understand, track, predict, and reduce their carbon footprint through AI-powered insights, automated data collection, and environmental storytelling.

---

## ✨ Vision

Most carbon footprint calculators tell users how much CO₂ they produce.

GreenMirror shows users what that impact looks like.

Our mission is to make sustainability personal, visible, and emotionally engaging by transforming environmental data into a living world that reflects everyday choices.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Understanding personal carbon emissions is difficult.

Existing solutions often suffer from:

* Complex data entry
* Generic recommendations
* Low user engagement
* Limited personalization
* Lack of emotional connection

As a result, users lose interest and rarely change their behavior.

GreenMirror addresses this by combining AI, behavioral design, and environmental visualization to create an experience users actually want to return to.

### 🤖 AI Carbon Coach

GreenMirror includes an AI-powered sustainability companion that provides personalized recommendations.

Examples:

> Taking public transport tomorrow could reduce emissions and improve air quality in your ecosystem.

> Reducing electricity consumption this evening may restore vegetation within your digital forest.

---

### 🧾 Smart Document Processing

Upload:

* Electricity bills
* Fuel receipts
* Grocery receipts

Using AI-powered OCR, GreenMirror automatically extracts relevant information and updates environmental metrics.

---

### 🔮 Future Impact Simulator

Explore two possible futures:

#### Current Path

Continue existing habits and observe projected environmental degradation.

#### Sustainable Path

Follow AI recommendations and visualize environmental recovery.

Users can compare outcomes and understand the long-term impact of their decisions.

---

### 🌱 Daily Environmental Reflection

At the end of each day, GreenMirror generates a visual summary of environmental progress.

Examples:

* Trees restored
* Butterflies returned
* River quality improved
* Pollution reduced

This creates a stronger emotional connection than traditional carbon reports.

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

GreenMirror is designed with privacy-first principles.

Features include:

* Firebase Authentication
* Secure cloud storage
* HTTPS encryption
* Encrypted data at rest and in transit
* Role-based access control
* User-controlled data deletion
* Data export functionality
* Minimal data collection
* No selling or sharing user information

Users maintain full control over their personal data.
